Design Concept: The Council's Synthesis

The geometric "M" lettermark incorporates subtle stethoscope negative space—honoring medical tradition while signaling modern innovation. The teal conveys trust and clinical precision; the coral adds warmth and human connection. This mark works at 16px (favicon) and billboard scale, solving the current inconsistency between stethoscope and heart icons. DoctorMX: Doctoralia, but better.

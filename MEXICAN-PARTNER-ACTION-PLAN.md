## **PHASE 1: LEGAL FOUNDATION (WEEKS 1-4)**

### **1.1 COFEPRIS Registration** 🏛️

#### **Week 1 Tasks:**
- [ ] **Contact COFEPRIS Regional Office**
  - Office: Guadalajara or Mexico City (closest to your location)
  - Phone: +52 55 5080 5200
  - Email: atencion.ciudadana@cofepris.gob.mx
  - **Request**: Medical Device Registration Package for AI Software

- [ ] **Gather Required Documentation**
  ```
  Required Documents:
  ✅ Medical Device Classification (Class IIa - Software for Medical Advice)
  ✅ Clinical validation studies (minimum 100 interactions)
  ✅ Risk management documentation (ISO 14971)
  ✅ Quality management system (ISO 13485)
  ✅ Software lifecycle processes (IEC 62304)
  ✅ Mexican Medical Board endorsement
  ```

- [ ] **Hire Regulatory Consultant**
  - **Recommended**: Regulatory Affairs Professionals Society (RAPS) Mexico
  - **Contact**: RAPS Mexico Chapter - raps.org/mexico
  - **Budget**: $50,000-100,000 MXN for full COFEPRIS submission
  - **Timeline**: 60-90 days for approval

#### **Week 2-4 Tasks:**
- [ ] **Prepare Clinical Evidence**
  - Document AI accuracy studies
  - Compile physician validation data
  - Prepare safety and efficacy reports
  - **Reference**: See `MEXICAN-HEALTHCARE-REGULATIONS.md` Section 1.1

- [ ] **Submit COFEPRIS Application**
  - Complete Registro Sanitario de Dispositivos Médicos
  - Pay registration fees (~$30,000 MXN)
  - Schedule inspection if required

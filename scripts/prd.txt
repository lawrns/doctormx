# DoctorMX Digital Marketing Automation Strategy PRD
## Executive Summary

### Product Vision
Create a comprehensive digital marketing automation system for DoctorMX, an AI-powered healthcare platform, to rapidly acquire 1,000+ users in 30 days and scale to 10,000+ monthly users within 90 days while maintaining compliance with Mexican healthcare regulations (COFEPRIS) and platform policies.

### Market Context
- Target: Mexican healthcare consumers seeking accessible, affordable medical consultations
- Primary competitors: Dr. Consulta, Doctoralia, Médica Sur Digital
- Unique positioning: AI-powered Mexican doctor (Dr. Simeon) with cultural understanding
- Key channels: WhatsApp (essential in Mexico), social media, content marketing, email automation

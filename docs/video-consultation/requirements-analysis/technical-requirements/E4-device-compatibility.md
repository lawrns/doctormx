## 📱 **Mobile Device Compatibility**

### **Android Device Requirements**

#### **Minimum Requirements (Tier 3 - Basic Support)**
- **OS Version**: Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher
- **RAM**: 2GB minimum
- **Storage**: 1GB available space
- **Camera**: 5MP rear camera, 2MP front camera
- **Processor**: Snapdragon 450 or equivalent (Quad-core 1.8GHz)
- **Network**: 4G LTE or WiFi 802.11n
- **Video Quality**: 480p at 15 FPS
- **Target Devices**: Budget smartphones, older devices

#### **Recommended Requirements (Tier 2 - Standard Support)**
- **OS Version**: Android 9.0 (API level 28) or higher
- **RAM**: 4GB minimum
- **Storage**: 2GB available space
- **Camera**: 12MP rear camera, 8MP front camera
- **Processor**: Snapdragon 660 or equivalent (Octa-core 2.2GHz)
- **Network**: 4G LTE Advanced or WiFi 802.11ac
- **Video Quality**: 720p at 30 FPS
- **Target Devices**: Mid-range smartphones (2019-2022)

#### **Optimal Requirements (Tier 1 - Premium Support)**
- **OS Version**: Android 11.0 (API level 30) or higher
- **RAM**: 6GB or more
- **Storage**: 4GB available space
- **Camera**: 48MP rear camera, 12MP front camera
- **Processor**: Snapdragon 855 or equivalent (Octa-core 2.8GHz+)
- **Network**: 5G or WiFi 802.11ax (WiFi 6)
- **Video Quality**: 1080p at 30 FPS, 4K capable
- **Target Devices**: Flagship smartphones (2020+)

### **iOS Device Requirements**

#### **Minimum Requirements (Tier 3 - Basic Support)**
- **Devices**: iPhone 7, iPad (6th generation) or newer
- **OS Version**: iOS 13.0 or higher
- **Storage**: 1GB available space
- **Camera**: 12MP rear camera, 7MP front camera
- **Processor**: A10 Bionic chip or newer
- **Network**: 4G LTE or WiFi 802.11n
- **Video Quality**: 720p at 30 FPS

#### **Recommended Requirements (Tier 2 - Standard Support)**
- **Devices**: iPhone X, iPad Pro (2018) or newer
- **OS Version**: iOS 14.0 or higher
- **Storage**: 2GB available space
- **Camera**: 12MP dual camera system
- **Processor**: A11 Bionic chip or newer
- **Network**: 4G LTE Advanced or WiFi 802.11ac
- **Video Quality**: 1080p at 30 FPS

#### **Optimal Requirements (Tier 1 - Premium Support)**
- **Devices**: iPhone 12, iPad Pro (2020) or newer
- **OS Version**: iOS 15.0 or higher
- **Storage**: 4GB available space
- **Camera**: 12MP Pro camera system
- **Processor**: A14 Bionic chip or newer
- **Network**: 5G or WiFi 6
- **Video Quality**: 4K at 30 FPS

## 🔧 **Technical Compatibility Requirements**

### **Network Connectivity**

#### **Internet Speed Requirements**
- **Minimum**: 1 Mbps download, 512 kbps upload
- **Recommended**: 5 Mbps download, 2 Mbps upload
- **Optimal**: 25 Mbps download, 10 Mbps upload
- **Latency**: <150ms for optimal experience
- **Packet Loss**: <1% for stable connections

#### **Network Types**
- **WiFi**: 802.11n minimum, 802.11ac recommended
- **Mobile Data**: 4G LTE minimum, 5G preferred
- **Wired**: Ethernet connection for desktop users
- **Satellite**: Supported with quality limitations

### **Hardware Acceleration**

#### **GPU Requirements**
- **Minimum**: Integrated graphics (Intel HD 4000, AMD Radeon R5)
- **Recommended**: Dedicated graphics (GTX 1050, RX 560)
- **Optimal**: Modern dedicated graphics (RTX 3060, RX 6600)
- **Hardware Encoding**: H.264 hardware encoding support
- **Hardware Decoding**: H.264/H.265 hardware decoding

#### **Audio Hardware**
- **Microphone**: Built-in or external microphone
- **Speakers/Headphones**: Stereo audio output
- **Echo Cancellation**: Hardware or software AEC support
- **Noise Suppression**: Background noise reduction capability

## 🎯 **Objective**
Define comprehensive revenue models for video consultation platform in Mexico, analyzing monetization strategies, pricing structures, and financial projections based on market analysis and consultation types.

## 💰 **Primary Revenue Models**

### **Model 1: Transaction-Based Revenue (Pay-Per-Consultation)**

#### **Structure**
- **Platform Commission**: 15-25% of each consultation fee
- **Doctor Keeps**: 75-85% of consultation revenue
- **Payment Processing**: Additional 3-5% for payment gateway
- **No Monthly Fees**: Doctors pay only when they earn

#### **Pricing by Consultation Type**
| Consultation Type | Doctor Fee | Platform Commission (20%) | Doctor Net |
|-------------------|------------|---------------------------|------------|
| **Follow-up** | $400-800 MXN | $80-160 MXN | $320-640 MXN |
| **Initial** | $600-1,200 MXN | $120-240 MXN | $480-960 MXN |
| **Specialist** | $1,000-2,000 MXN | $200-400 MXN | $800-1,600 MXN |
| **Emergency Triage** | $300-600 MXN | $60-120 MXN | $240-480 MXN |
| **Preventive** | $500-800 MXN | $100-160 MXN | $400-640 MXN |

#### **Advantages**
- **Low Barrier to Entry**: No upfront costs for doctors
- **Scalable**: Revenue grows with platform usage
- **Risk Sharing**: Platform success tied to doctor success
- **Market Penetration**: Easy adoption for hesitant doctors

#### **Disadvantages**
- **Revenue Volatility**: Dependent on consultation volume
- **Doctor Resistance**: Some may prefer fixed costs
- **Complex Accounting**: Variable revenue tracking required

#### **Target Segments**
- **New Doctors**: Starting telemedicine practice
- **Part-time Users**: Occasional video consultations
- **Small Practices**: Limited patient volume

---

### **Model 2: Subscription-Based Revenue (SaaS Model)**

#### **Tier Structure**

##### **🥉 Basic Plan - $2,500 MXN/month**
- **Consultation Limit**: Up to 50 consultations/month
- **Features**: Standard video quality, basic EMR integration
- **Support**: Email support, knowledge base access
- **Commission**: 10% on consultations
- **Target**: Solo practitioners, part-time telemedicine

##### **🥈 Professional Plan - $5,000 MXN/month**
- **Consultation Limit**: Up to 150 consultations/month
- **Features**: HD video, full EMR integration, prescription management
- **Support**: Priority email + phone support
- **Commission**: 5% on consultations
- **Target**: Established practices, growing telemedicine adoption

##### **🥇 Enterprise Plan - $10,000 MXN/month**
- **Consultation Limit**: Unlimited consultations
- **Features**: 4K video, advanced analytics, API access, white-label options
- **Support**: Dedicated account manager, 24/7 support
- **Commission**: 0% on consultations
- **Target**: Large practices, hospitals, medical institutions

#### **Advantages**
- **Predictable Revenue**: Stable monthly recurring revenue
- **Higher LTV**: Long-term customer relationships
- **Premium Features**: Ability to offer advanced functionality
- **Scalability**: Clear upgrade path for growing practices

#### **Disadvantages**
- **Higher Barrier**: Upfront monthly commitment
- **Churn Risk**: Monthly cancellation possibility
- **Feature Complexity**: Need to justify subscription value

---

### **Model 3: Hybrid Revenue Model (Recommended)**

#### **Structure Combination**
- **Base Subscription**: Lower monthly fee for platform access
- **Transaction Fee**: Reduced commission on consultations
- **Premium Add-ons**: Additional features for extra cost

##### **🎯 Starter Hybrid - $1,500 MXN/month + 15% commission**
- **Consultation Limit**: Up to 30 consultations/month
- **Features**: Standard platform access, basic support
- **Overage**: $50 MXN per consultation above limit
- **Target**: New telemedicine adopters

##### **🎯 Growth Hybrid - $3,500 MXN/month + 10% commission**
- **Consultation Limit**: Up to 100 consultations/month
- **Features**: Advanced features, priority support
- **Overage**: $35 MXN per consultation above limit
- **Target**: Growing practices

##### **🎯 Scale Hybrid - $7,500 MXN/month + 5% commission**
- **Consultation Limit**: Up to 300 consultations/month
- **Features**: Enterprise features, dedicated support
- **Overage**: $25 MXN per consultation above limit
- **Target**: Large practices, institutions

#### **Advantages**
- **Balanced Risk**: Combines predictable and variable revenue
- **Flexible Adoption**: Lower barrier than pure subscription
- **Growth Alignment**: Revenue scales with doctor success
- **Market Coverage**: Appeals to different practice sizes

## 🎯 **Pricing Strategy Analysis**

### **Competitive Positioning**

#### **Premium Positioning (Recommended)**
- **Price Point**: 15-25% above competitors
- **Value Proposition**: Superior technology, better support, comprehensive features
- **Target Market**: Quality-focused doctors, established practices
- **Justification**: Higher quality platform, better patient outcomes

#### **Market Penetration Alternative**
- **Price Point**: 10-15% below competitors
- **Value Proposition**: Best value, comprehensive features at lower cost
- **Target Market**: Price-sensitive doctors, new telemedicine adopters
- **Risk**: Lower margins, potential quality perception issues

### **Dynamic Pricing Opportunities**

#### **Geographic Pricing**
- **Tier 1 Cities** (CDMX, Guadalajara, Monterrey): Standard pricing
- **Tier 2 Cities**: 15% discount
- **Rural Areas**: 25% discount + government subsidies

#### **Specialty-Based Pricing**
- **High-Value Specialties** (Cardiology, Dermatology): Premium pricing
- **Primary Care**: Standard pricing
- **Mental Health**: Social impact pricing (10% discount)

#### **Volume Discounts**
- **Individual Doctors**: Standard rates
- **Group Practices** (5+ doctors): 10% discount
- **Hospitals/Institutions** (20+ doctors): 20% discount

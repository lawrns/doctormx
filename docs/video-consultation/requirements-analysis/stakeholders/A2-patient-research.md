## 🚧 **Technology Barriers Identified**

### **Infrastructure Barriers**
- **Poor Internet Connectivity**: Rural areas, data costs
- **Device Limitations**: Older smartphones, small screens
- **Bandwidth Issues**: Video quality concerns

### **Skills Barriers**
- **Digital Literacy**: Older adults, rural populations
- **Language Barriers**: Indigenous languages, technical terms
- **Interface Complexity**: Need for simple, intuitive design

### **Economic Barriers**
- **Data Costs**: Mobile internet expensive for low-income users
- **Device Costs**: Upgrading to video-capable devices
- **Service Costs**: Affordability of video consultations

## 🏥 **Healthcare Behavior Patterns**

### **Current Healthcare Usage**
- **Public System (IMSS/ISSSTE)**: 66.7% primary usage
- **Private Care**: 50% occasional usage
- **Mixed Approach**: Most use combination based on urgency/quality needs

### **Decision Factors**
1. **Cost**: Primary concern for 83.3% of personas
2. **Convenience**: Important for urban professionals
3. **Quality**: Balanced with cost considerations
4. **Trust**: Critical for adoption, especially older adults

### **Pain Points**
- **Wait Times**: Universal complaint across all personas
- **Transportation**: Especially rural and elderly
- **Work Schedule Conflicts**: Working adults
- **Language/Cultural Barriers**: Rural and indigenous populations

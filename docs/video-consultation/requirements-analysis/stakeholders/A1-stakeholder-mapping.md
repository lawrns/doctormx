## 🎯 **Objective**
Identify and analyze all stakeholders involved in the video consultation system implementation, mapping their power, influence, and engagement requirements.

## 📊 **Deliverables Required**

### **1. Stakeholder Identification Matrix**

| Stakeholder Type | Description | Primary Interest | Impact Level | Influence Level |
|------------------|-------------|------------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Patients** | End users seeking medical consultation | Convenient, affordable healthcare access | High | Medium |
| **Doctors** | Medical professionals providing consultations | Efficient practice, patient care quality | High | High |
| **Medical Institutions** | Hospitals, clinics, medical centers | Integration with existing systems | High | High |
| **Regulatory Bodies** | COFEPRIS, Health Ministry (SSA) | Compliance with telemedicine regulations | High | Very High |
| **Insurance Companies** | Health insurance providers | Cost control, coverage policies | Medium | High |
| **Technology Partners** | Video platform providers (Agora, WebRTC) | Platform adoption, technical integration | Medium | Medium |
| **Legal/Compliance Team** | Internal legal and compliance specialists | Risk mitigation, regulatory adherence | High | High |
| **Development Team** | Internal developers and architects | Technical feasibility, implementation | High | Medium |
| **Business Stakeholders** | Product managers, business analysts | ROI, market success | High | High |
| **Support Staff** | Customer service, technical support | User experience, issue resolution | Medium | Low |

### **2. Power/Influence Analysis**

#### **High Power, High Influence (Manage Closely)**
- **Regulatory Bodies (COFEPRIS, SSA)**: Critical for legal operation
- **Medical Institutions**: Essential for adoption and credibility
- **Doctors**: Core users whose acceptance determines success

#### **High Power, Medium Influence (Keep Satisfied)**
- **Insurance Companies**: Can drive or block adoption through coverage
- **Legal/Compliance Team**: Can halt project if concerns arise

#### **Medium Power, High Influence (Keep Informed)**
- **Business Stakeholders**: Drive strategic decisions and funding
- **Development Team**: Technical implementation capability

#### **Low Power, High Influence (Monitor)**
- **Patients**: End users whose satisfaction drives success
- **Technology Partners**: Platform reliability affects user experience

### **3. Champions & Detractors Analysis**

#### **Potential Champions**
- **Progressive Doctors**: Tech-savvy physicians seeking efficiency
- **Business Stakeholders**: Focused on market opportunity and ROI
- **Development Team**: Excited about technical innovation
- **Young Patients**: Comfortable with digital health solutions

#### **Potential Detractors**
- **Traditional Doctors**: May resist technology adoption
- **Regulatory Bodies**: Cautious about new telemedicine implementations
- **Older Patients**: May prefer in-person consultations
- **Insurance Companies**: May resist covering video consultations initially

#### **Neutral/Undecided**
- **Medical Institutions**: Will evaluate based on proven results
- **Support Staff**: Will adapt based on training and tools provided

### **4. Communication Plan**

#### **Regulatory Bodies (COFEPRIS, SSA)**
- **Frequency**: Monthly formal updates
- **Method**: Official documentation, compliance reports
- **Key Messages**: Regulatory adherence, patient safety, data protection
- **Success Metrics**: Approval timelines, compliance scores

#### **Doctors**
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly during development, weekly during pilot
- **Method**: Medical advisory board, surveys, focus groups
- **Key Messages**: Improved efficiency, patient care quality, ease of use
- **Success Metrics**: Adoption rate, satisfaction scores, usage frequency

#### **Patients**
- **Frequency**: Monthly surveys, continuous feedback collection
- **Method**: User research, beta testing, support channels
- **Key Messages**: Convenience, accessibility, quality care
- **Success Metrics**: User satisfaction, retention rate, referral rate

#### **Medical Institutions**
- **Frequency**: Quarterly business reviews
- **Method**: Executive presentations, pilot programs, case studies
- **Key Messages**: Integration capabilities, ROI, competitive advantage
- **Success Metrics**: Partnership agreements, integration success

#### **Business Stakeholders**
- **Frequency**: Weekly status updates, monthly strategic reviews
- **Method**: Dashboard reports, executive meetings, milestone reviews
- **Key Messages**: Progress against goals, market opportunity, risk mitigation
- **Success Metrics**: Timeline adherence, budget compliance, KPI achievement

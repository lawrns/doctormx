## 🌐 **Platform Legal Documents**

### **Terms of Service Agreement**

#### **Service Description & Scope**
```
DOCTORMX VIDEO CONSULTATION TERMS OF SERVICE

1. SERVICE DESCRIPTION
DoctorMX provides a digital platform connecting patients with licensed medical 
professionals for video consultations. Our services include:
- Video consultation scheduling and delivery
- Secure medical record management
- Electronic prescription services
- Payment processing and billing

2. ELIGIBILITY & REGISTRATION
- Patients: 18+ years old or legal guardian consent for minors
- Doctors: Valid Mexican medical license and professional liability insurance
- Account verification required for all users
- Accurate information required for registration

3. PERMITTED USES
- Legitimate medical consultations only
- Compliance with all applicable laws and regulations
- Respect for intellectual property rights
- Professional conduct in all interactions

4. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES
- Emergency medical situations (call 911 immediately)
- Prescription drug abuse or illegal drug requests
- Sharing account credentials or unauthorized access
- Harassment, discrimination, or unprofessional conduct
```

#### **Medical Disclaimer & Limitations**
```
5. MEDICAL DISCLAIMER
- Video consultations have inherent limitations compared to in-person visits
- Not suitable for emergency medical situations
- Doctors may require in-person examination for proper diagnosis
- Platform does not guarantee medical outcomes
- Patients responsible for following medical advice

6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
- Platform liability limited to service fees paid
- No liability for medical malpractice (doctor responsibility)
- Force majeure events exclude platform liability
- Consequential damages excluded to maximum extent permitted by law
```

### **Privacy Policy**

#### **Data Collection & Processing**
```
DOCTORMX PRIVACY POLICY

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
Personal Information:
- Name, date of birth, contact information
- Medical history and health information
- Payment and billing information
- Device and usage information

Technical Information:
- IP address, browser type, device identifiers
- Video call quality and connection data
- Platform usage analytics and performance metrics

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
- Provide video consultation services
- Maintain medical records and consultation history
- Process payments and billing
- Improve platform performance and user experience
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. INFORMATION SHARING
We share information only:
- With your healthcare providers for treatment
- With service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
- When required by law or regulatory authorities
- With your explicit consent for specific purposes
```

#### **Patient Rights & Data Protection**
```
4. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS
- Access: Request copies of your personal information
- Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
- Deletion: Request deletion of your information (with medical exceptions)
- Portability: Request transfer of your information
- Objection: Object to certain processing activities

5. DATA SECURITY
- End-to-end encryption for all communications
- Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Staff training on data protection and privacy

6. DATA RETENTION
- Medical records: 5 years minimum (Mexican law requirement)
- Payment information: 7 years for tax compliance
- Marketing data: Until consent withdrawn
- Technical logs: 2 years for security and performance analysis
```

## 👨‍⚕️ **Medical Professional Agreements**

### **Doctor Service Agreement**

#### **Professional Requirements & Obligations**
```
DOCTORMX DOCTOR SERVICE AGREEMENT

1. PROFESSIONAL QUALIFICATIONS
Doctor represents and warrants:
- Valid and current Mexican medical license
- Professional liability insurance ($2M minimum coverage)
- Good standing with medical regulatory authorities
- Completion of telemedicine training and certification

2. SERVICE OBLIGATIONS
- Provide professional medical consultations via video platform
- Maintain patient confidentiality and medical ethics
- Complete accurate and timely medical documentation
- Respond to patient communications within 24 hours
- Participate in quality assurance and peer review programs

3. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
- Comply with all applicable medical laws and regulations
- Follow platform clinical guidelines and protocols
- Maintain continuing medical education requirements
- Report adverse events and patient safety concerns
- Cooperate with regulatory audits and investigations
```

#### **Compensation & Payment Terms**
```
4. COMPENSATION STRUCTURE
- Base consultation fees as published on platform
- Platform commission: 15-20% of consultation fees
- Payment processing: 3-5% of gross fees
- Monthly payment via direct deposit
- Detailed revenue reporting and tax documentation

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
- Doctor retains rights to medical expertise and methods
- Platform owns technology, software, and platform improvements
- Shared ownership of anonymized medical data for research
- Confidentiality of platform proprietary information

6. TERMINATION
- Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice
- Immediate termination for breach of agreement or professional misconduct
- Post-termination obligations: patient record transfer, confidentiality
- Return of platform property and confidential information
```

## 👥 **Patient Consent & Agreements**

### **Informed Consent for Telemedicine**

#### **Telemedicine Consent Form**
```
INFORMED CONSENT FOR TELEMEDICINE SERVICES

I understand and agree to the following:

1. NATURE OF TELEMEDICINE
- Video consultations have benefits and limitations
- Some medical conditions require in-person examination
- Technology failures may interrupt consultations
- Emergency situations require immediate in-person care

2. PRIVACY AND CONFIDENTIALITY
- Video consultations are private and confidential
- Recordings may be made for quality assurance (with consent)
- Medical information will be protected according to privacy laws
- I am responsible for ensuring privacy of my consultation location

3. TECHNOLOGY REQUIREMENTS
- Reliable internet connection required for video consultations
- Compatible device with camera and microphone
- Private location for confidential medical discussion
- Technical support available for connection issues

4. PATIENT RESPONSIBILITIES
- Provide accurate medical history and current symptoms
- Follow prescribed treatment plans and medication instructions
- Seek immediate in-person care for emergencies
- Notify doctor of any adverse reactions or complications
```

#### **Data Processing Consent**
```
5. CONSENT FOR DATA PROCESSING
I consent to the collection, use, and processing of my health information for:
- Providing telemedicine services and medical care
- Maintaining medical records and consultation history
- Quality improvement and patient safety programs
- Regulatory compliance and reporting requirements

I understand my rights regarding my personal health information:
- Right to access and review my medical records
- Right to request corrections to inaccurate information
- Right to restrict certain uses of my information
- Right to file complaints about privacy practices

6. CONSENT WITHDRAWAL
- I may withdraw this consent at any time in writing
- Withdrawal does not affect previously authorized uses
- Some uses may continue as required by law
- Withdrawal may limit my ability to receive telemedicine services
```

### **Minor Patient Consent**

#### **Parental/Guardian Consent**
```
CONSENT FOR MINOR PATIENT TELEMEDICINE SERVICES

As parent/legal guardian of [Minor's Name], I provide consent for:

1. TELEMEDICINE SERVICES
- Video consultations between minor and licensed physicians
- Medical history collection and health assessment
- Treatment recommendations and prescription services
- Medical record creation and maintenance

2. PARENTAL INVOLVEMENT
- Parent/guardian may be present during consultations
- Medical information may be shared with parent/guardian
- Treatment decisions require parental approval for minors under 16
- Emergency contact information must be current and accurate

3. CONFIDENTIALITY CONSIDERATIONS
- Adolescent patients (16-17) may have private consultation time
- Certain sensitive health topics may require confidentiality
- Mental health and reproductive health consultations may be private
- Life-threatening situations will be disclosed to parents immediately
```

## 🤝 **Business Agreements**

### **Vendor & Service Provider Agreements**

#### **Business Associate Agreement (BAA)**
```
BUSINESS ASSOCIATE AGREEMENT

This agreement ensures HIPAA compliance for all vendors handling protected health information:

1. PERMITTED USES AND DISCLOSURES
Business Associate may use and disclose PHI only:
- To perform services specified in the underlying agreement
- For proper management and administration of Business Associate
- To carry out legal responsibilities of Business Associate
- As required by law

2. SAFEGUARDS AND SECURITY
Business Associate must:
- Implement appropriate safeguards to protect PHI
- Report any unauthorized use or disclosure of PHI
- Ensure subcontractors comply with same restrictions
- Return or destroy PHI upon termination of agreement

3. INDIVIDUAL RIGHTS
Business Associate must:
- Provide access to PHI for amendment by individuals
- Make PHI available for accounting of disclosures
- Designate privacy officer for PHI-related matters
- Cooperate with covered entity's compliance efforts
```

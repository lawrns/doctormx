## 🇲🇽 **Mexican Healthcare Regulatory Framework**

### **Primary Regulatory Bodies**

#### **COFEPRIS (Comisión Federal para la Protección contra Riesgos Sanitarios)**
- **Role**: Medical device regulation and healthcare technology oversight
- **Jurisdiction**: Telemedicine platform classification and approval
- **Requirements**: Medical device registration, quality management systems
- **Timeline**: 6-12 months for approval process
- **Cost**: $15,000-50,000 USD for registration and compliance

#### **Secretaría de Salud (SSA)**
- **Role**: National health policy and healthcare service regulation
- **Jurisdiction**: Telemedicine service authorization and standards
- **Requirements**: Healthcare service provider registration
- **Timeline**: 3-6 months for authorization
- **Cost**: $5,000-15,000 USD for registration

#### **Instituto Federal de Telecomunicaciones (IFT)**
- **Role**: Telecommunications and digital services regulation
- **Jurisdiction**: Digital platform operations and data transmission
- **Requirements**: Telecommunications service provider registration
- **Timeline**: 2-4 months for registration
- **Cost**: $10,000-25,000 USD annually

### **Key Regulatory Requirements**

#### **Telemedicine Service Authorization**
- **Legal Framework**: NOM-024-SSA3-2012 (Telemedicine Standards)
- **Requirements**: 
  - Licensed medical professionals only
  - Secure patient data transmission
  - Medical record maintenance (5 years minimum)
  - Emergency escalation protocols
  - Quality assurance programs

#### **Medical Device Classification**
- **Classification**: Class II Medical Device Software
- **Requirements**:
  - ISO 13485 Quality Management System
  - ISO 14971 Risk Management
  - IEC 62304 Medical Device Software Lifecycle
  - Clinical evaluation and post-market surveillance

#### **Data Protection Compliance**
- **Legal Framework**: Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales (LFPDPPP)
- **Requirements**:
  - Explicit patient consent for data processing
  - Data minimization and purpose limitation
  - Security measures for sensitive health data
  - Data breach notification (72 hours)
  - Patient rights (access, rectification, deletion)
